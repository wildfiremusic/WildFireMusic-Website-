About Us

We are WildFire. Musicians with dreams. Aspirations. Hopes. People who create music out of passion. A passion for making people happy, making people dance, and getting tunes stuck in peoples’ heads. That’s the most eloquent way to put it, but in reality, we’re a bunch of teenagers slapping stuff together on Logic Pro X as often as we can. Our team of talented musicians and managers make this a reality.
